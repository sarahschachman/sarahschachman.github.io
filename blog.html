Core Values

People first

My passion for people is what sparked my interest in design. While making pretty things is lovely, my goal is to create products that people fall in love with. I want to make their lives easier and more fun by innovating in fields that need some new ideas. Humans are all about connection -- I think the next step is making technology about human connection as well.

Iterate fast

Though I tend to be meticulous and detail-oriented, my drive to get things done keeps me focused on the big picture. My experience in leadership positions and event-planning has made me extremely organized and deadline-driven, so I like to keep a tight loop between design and feedback.

Communicate ideas

When I design, I am constantly asking myself why -- why should I design an element this way, or another way? Questioning myself opens me up to better solutions, and being confident in my decisions helps me reason about and defend my designs.

My unique perspectives

My atypical path has given me unique perspectives to bring to the table when designing innovative, intuitive products.
Form global networks

Growing up in different countries and completing tech internships in two international locations has given me the cross-cultural perspective to design concepts that extend across communities, and the goal of connecting people across the globe. You can learn more about my experience here.

Put People first

My passion for people is what sparked my interest in design. My goal is to create useful, usable, and unique products that people fall in love with. I want to make their lives easier through innovation. Humans are all about connection; I think the next step is making technology about human connection as well.

Understand the mind

As someone who studies both computer science and psychology, I've learned how to design better solutions based upon a deep understanding of the human mind. Being confident in the psychological premise behind my design decisions helps me reason about and defend my work.

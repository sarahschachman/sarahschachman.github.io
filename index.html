About me

I'm Sarah, a junior at Harvard studying Computer Science and Psychology.

Originally from Montana, Georgia, Japan, Spain, North Carolina (and a few others - #militarylife), it's not hard to understand why I've got the travel bug. I love exploring new cultures and beautiful places with rich history. I first realized my passsion for design while spending the summer in Cape Town, South Africa, a vibrant city with an abundance of innovators. Other things I enjoy doing are hiking, reading, country swing dancing, and ostrich riding.

Harvard Laundry App

Design

This a project I've been working on as a member of Springboard, Harvard's design team. Our team's goal was to streamline the laundry process here at Harvard, which can be a tedious and time-consuming endeavor. We will integrate a new section into the existing Harvard App, so that people can pay for their laundry and activate the machines using their Crimson Cash. They will also get reminders when their laundry is done, and be able to see all the machines currently running. The project is currently in the prototyping phase.

Teaching and Learning Technologies

Rails App

I am currently working on a project proposed by a physician studying information retention and studying habits. I am designing and implementing a student-generated spaced education platform for students to synthesize things they’ve learned in class by writing their own questions and answering questions of other students. The product will be comprised of two communicating Rails apps -- one serves as the App that students will interact with, and the other is a database that will store questions. Later on in the project, I will be using LTI to integrate the app into Canvas, our school’s course site platform.

UX Research

Redefining Retail

Overview

Currently in progress! Sept 4 - now

The Innovator’s Practice is a Harvard course aimed at breaking down the process of human centered design and creating opportunities for real-world experience. I am working in a group with three other students from both the college and the Graduate School of Design, and have chosen to focus on the shopping experience in fashion. We found a common interest in fashion and decided to see what pain points we could uncover in the shopping process, whether that be in-store, online, or through a subscription service. We wanted to see what the motivations were behind choosing one channel over the other, and how the channel affects the shopper’s experience. Our hope going into the project was to get to the core of the shopping experience and uncover what it is missing, and where we might be able to innovate and redefine retail.
